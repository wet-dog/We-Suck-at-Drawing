How do I play?

Once you join a room you need to draw a picture for yourself. Once everyone is in the lobby you can press the Everybody's Ready button. At this point, everyone should check their devices for a drawing prompt. You need to do your best to draw your prompt. Once the drawing phase is over the game will take turns showing everyones artwork. Your job is to come up with a prompt that is simmular to what the drawing looks like. The game will post everyones answers, your goal is to trick people into thinking your lie is the correct prompt while you find the actual answer. We assign points based on answers and and after 2 full rounds the player with the most points wins.
